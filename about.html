About

Smart Intersection is a real-time simulation of an AI-managed crossroads. Instead of traffic lights or stop signs, an intelligent controller watches every approaching car and calculates the exact velocity each one needs to pass through the intersection without any collisions.

Think of it as air traffic control for roads — every car gets a personalized clearance window with a guaranteed safe-passage velocity. The result is mesmerizing: dozens of cars weaving through each other at full speed with perfect coordination.

How It Works

📡

Detection

When a car enters the approach zone (250px from center), it sends a clearance request to the intersection controller.

🧮

Reservation

The AI calculates safe entry/exit time windows and assigns a velocity that avoids all existing reservations in the zone.

⏱️

Timing

Each reservation includes precise enter and exit times with a 0.2s safety gap between consecutive crossings.

🎯

Velocity Control

Cleared cars adjust speed to arrive at exactly the right moment — speeding up or slowing down as calculated.

🔄

U-Turn Loops

Cars travel in continuous loops — horizontal or vertical paths with U-turn arcs at each end of the road.

🔍

Forensics

When collisions occur, a detailed forensic report shows reservation overlaps, timing drifts, and car snapshots.

Vehicle Fleet

Each car is assigned a unique color from the simulation palette. Cars vary in base speed (55–90 units) and follow either horizontal or vertical loop paths.

↔️

Horizontal Lanes

Cars travel left-to-right and right-to-left, performing U-turns at screen edges. Add more with the + Horiz button.

↕️

Vertical Lanes

Cars travel top-to-bottom and bottom-to-top, crossing perpendicular to horizontal traffic. Add with + Vert.

➖

Remove Cars

The − Car button removes cars from the simulation, reducing intersection complexity in real time.

Features

🤖

AI Controller

No traffic lights — the intersection manager assigns collision-free velocities using temporal reservation slots.

🚗

Dynamic Lanes

Add horizontal and vertical lanes in real-time. Watch the AI adapt to increasingly complex traffic patterns.

📊

Live HUD

Real-time statistics: car count, throughput, negotiations, collisions, rear-ends, stalls, near misses, and average delay.

🔲

Hitbox Mode

Toggle OBB (Oriented Bounding Box) visualization to see exactly how collision detection works.

⏸️

Auto-Pause on Crash

Enable automatic pause when a collision is detected, freezing the scene for detailed forensic analysis.

🔬

Collision Forensics

Detailed crash reports showing reservation timings, car states, positions, speeds, and diagnostic data.

📋

Event Log

Scrolling log of all system events: clearances granted, near misses detected, collisions reported.

✨

Particle Effects

Visual particle bursts at the intersection zone when cars pass through or collide.
